## 1. Innovate UK — Textile Innovation Funding

### Innovate UK Textile Fund
- Part of the **£15M UKRI Circular Fashion and Textile Programme**
- Delivered by LITAC (Leeds Institute of Textiles and Colour) and Future Fashion Factory
- Projects: 4-6 months, total eligible costs up to £50,000
- Industrial partners can apply for **100% of eligible project costs**

### Eligibility Requirements
- Lead industrial partner must have **textile design or textile manufacturing as core business** OR have an **innovative application of technology that adds value to textile industries**
- Lead partner must have turnover of at least **£50,000 per year**
- Must be an established UK business

### Is Maison Move Eligible?
**Not yet for the Textile Fund specifically** — you need £50K annual turnover first. However:
- Your fabric technology innovation (SculptMap compression mapping, MineralSoft recovery fabric, ClimateKnit PCM) could qualify as "innovative application of technology that adds value to textile industries"
- **Action:** Register your business first, build turnover through DTC sales, then apply in Year 2
- **In the meantime:** Check the Innovate UK competition portal regularly for open calls — they run different competitions throughout the year with varying eligibility

### Where to Check
- **Competition portal:** apply-for-innovation-funding.service.gov.uk/competition/search
- **UKFT (UK Fashion & Textiles):** ukft.org/resources/investment-and-funding
- **Innovate UK Business Connect:** iuk-business-connect.org.uk

## 2. Start Up Loans (Government-Backed)

### What It Is
- Government-backed personal loan for new businesses
- Delivered through the British Business Bank
- **Up to £25,000 per founder**
- Fixed interest rate of 6% per year
- Repayment over 1-5 years
- **Free mentoring included** for 12 months after the loan

### Eligibility
- UK resident aged 18+
- Business must be based in the UK
- Business must be under 3 years old (or pre-launch)
- Must have a viable business plan
- Personal loan (secured against you, not the business)

### Is Maison Move Eligible?
**Yes — this is your most accessible funding option right now.**
- You can apply pre-launch
- £25,000 would cover your first production run, sampling, branding, and initial marketing
- The free 12-month mentoring is valuable for a first-time founder

### How to Apply
- Visit startuploans.co.uk
- Complete an online application
- Submit a business plan (you already have the brand bible — expand it with financials)
- Decision typically within a few weeks

## 3. Other Relevant Grants & Funding

### The Prince's Trust Enterprise Programme
- For founders aged 18-30
- Grants of up to £1,000-£5,000 (varies)
- Free business mentoring and support
- **Eligible:** Yes — you're 24 and starting a business
- Website: princes-trust.org.uk

### Zero-Waste Fashion and Textile Innovation Grant
- Up to £7,600
- For UK fashion/textile businesses focused on sustainability
- Your small-batch, low-waste production model could qualify
- Check eligibility on the grant portal

### Local Enterprise Partnership (LEP) Grants
- Varies by region (Wales has Development Bank of Wales)
- Small grants (£1,000-£10,000) for local startups
- **Development Bank of Wales** offers micro loans from £1,000 to £50,000
- Website: developmentbank.wales

### R&D Tax Credits
- Not a grant but a tax relief
- Claim back up to 33% of qualifying R&D expenditure
- Your fabric technology development (testing compression zones, mineral treatments, PCM integration) likely qualifies as R&D
- **You can claim this from your first year of trading**
- Speak to an accountant who specialises in R&D tax credits for startups

### UKRI Innovation Loans
- For businesses with fewer than 500 employees
- Loans of £100,000 to £2M
- For "highly innovative projects with strong commercial potential"
- **Future option** once you have proven sales and want to scale technology development
