Our Story

Why We Exist.

We started Maison Move because we couldn't find activewear that matched how we actually live. We move between the gym floor, the school run, brunch, and the boardroom — and we refuse to change four times to do it. Every piece in the collection is designed to cross those boundaries invisibly: technical enough to train in, beautiful enough to be seen in. We believe performance fabric and fashion-house design aren't opposites. They're overdue partners.

Sapphira Mort

Founder

Fabric-First

We begin every design with the material, not the mood board. If the fabric can't perform, the garment doesn't ship.

Engineered, Not Decorated

Every seam, panel, and cut line exists for a reason. Structure where you need support, freedom where you need movement.

Studio to Street

Designed to cross contexts without changing. From the reformer to the restaurant, nothing about you looks out of place.

Quiet Luxury

No logos on display. No trend-chasing. Just exceptional fit, premium hand-feel, and details only you'll notice.
