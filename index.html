The Innovation

Three proprietary technologies. One philosophy: your body deserves engineering, not marketing.

01

BodyMap™

Multi-Zone Adaptive Compression

Three to four strategically placed compression zones mapped to the body. Higher compression around the core and glutes for support and sculpting. Lighter compression at the calves and arms for freedom of movement. Breathable zones at high-heat areas. Designed with input from sports physiotherapists.

02

MineralSoft™

Recovery-Infused Fabric

Mineral-infused microcapsules release trace minerals through skin contact. Far-infrared yarn technology reflects body heat back as restorative energy. Anti-microbial silver-ion properties keep the garment fresh between washes. Wrapped in a matte luxury finish that looks as expensive as it performs.

03

ClimateKnit™

Temperature-Regulating Technology

Phase-change material microcapsules embedded in the fabric absorb body heat during intense exercise and release it when you cool down. This technology exists in outdoor and military gear — Maison Move brings it to premium women's activewear.

Interactive

BodyMap™ Zones

Hover over each zone to explore how multi-zone adaptive compression works across the body.

Hover over the highlighted zones on the body to discover the compression mapping.

High Compression

Core & Waist

Maximum support around the core for sculpting and posture. Engineered to hold without restricting breathing. The fabric compresses evenly across the midsection, reducing muscle oscillation during high-impact movement.

High Compression

Glutes & Hips

Targeted lift and sculpt compression around the glutes. The seam architecture follows the natural contour of the muscle, providing support during squats, lunges, and deadlifts without riding or shifting.

Light Compression

Calves & Ankles

Graduated light compression for freedom of movement. Enough support to reduce fatigue during long sessions, light enough to feel unrestricted. The fabric weight drops to 180 GSM in this zone for breathability.

Breathable Zone

Behind the Knees

Mesh-ventilated panels at high-heat areas. These zones use an open-knit structure that increases airflow by 40% compared to standard compression fabric. Heat escapes, comfort stays.

Why We Exist.

Maison Move was born from a problem I lived every day. I'd finish a weights session and have coffee with friends straight after — but nothing I owned could do both. I was coming home to change between every part of my day. Gym, then home. Change. Go out, then home. Change again. My wardrobe was split in two and my life was working around it.

The brands I loved did one thing well. Performance wear that looked like performance wear. Beautiful lounge pieces that couldn't survive a warm-up. Nothing existed in the middle — nothing that could take me from a deadlift to a flat white without a second thought.

So I built it. Every Maison Move piece is constructed with BodyMap™ multi-zone compression that genuinely supports your body through training, paired with our MineralSoft™ recovery fabric that feels buttery soft against the skin and helps your muscles restore while you wear it. The technology is invisible — what you feel is something that performs like your best gym kit but looks and feels elevated enough to wear anywhere.

We don't believe you should have to choose between how something performs and how it makes you feel. At Maison Move, we pride ourselves on being the best at both — because you shouldn't have to compromise, and neither should your clothes.

The house was built for the woman who refuses to pause.

Sapphira Mort Founder
