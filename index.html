01

SculptMap™

Most activewear applies uniform compression everywhere. SculptMap uses a zoned architecture, varying density, weight, and knit structure across each garment so the fabric responds differently depending on what your body needs in that area. Phase-change microcapsules regulate temperature throughout, absorbing excess heat during intense movement and releasing it as you cool. The contour seams aren't just aesthetic. They mark where the compression zones change.

02

MaisonModal™

A heavyweight modal-spandex blend with a brushed interior that feels like cashmere against the skin. Engineered for drape. Falls like water, holds its shape, and floats over the body without clinging. The fabric of rest. You train in SculptMap™. You reset in MaisonModal™.

Technology

SculptMap™

Four compression zones, engineered for how you move. Each zone is mapped to the body's natural mechanics. Supporting where you need it, releasing where you don't.

01

Core & Waist

High Compression

Maximum support around the core for sculpting and posture. Engineered to hold without restricting breathing. The fabric compresses evenly across the midsection, reducing muscle oscillation during high-impact movement.

02

Glutes & Hips

High Compression

Targeted lift and sculpt compression around the glutes. The seam architecture follows the natural contour of the muscle, providing support during squats, lunges, and deadlifts without riding or shifting.

03

Calves & Ankles

Light Compression

Graduated light compression for freedom of movement. Enough support to reduce fatigue during long sessions, light enough to feel unrestricted. The fabric weight drops to 180 GSM in this zone for breathability.

04

Behind the Knees

Breathable Zone

Mesh-ventilated panels at high-heat areas. These zones use an open-knit structure that increases airflow by 40% compared to standard compression fabric. Heat escapes, comfort stays.

Our Story

Why We Exist.

Every woman knows the feeling. You finish training, you have plans straight after, and nothing in your wardrobe works for both. Your clothes don't stay fresh after a session, and they certainly don't look good enough for what comes next.

The best performance brands deliver exactly that. Performance. Beautiful loungewear can't survive a warm-up. Nothing exists in the middle. Nothing that carries you from a deadlift to a flat white without a second thought.

Maison Move was created to close that gap. Every piece is constructed with SculptMap™ multi-zone compression that genuinely supports the body through training, paired with MaisonModal™ brushed modal blends that drape like water and feel like cashmere against the skin. The technology is invisible. What you feel is something that performs like your best gym kit but looks and feels elevated enough to wear anywhere.

There should never be a choice between how something performs and how it makes you feel. Maison Move is built to be the best at both. Because you shouldn't have to compromise, and neither should your clothes.

The house was built for the woman who refuses to pause.
