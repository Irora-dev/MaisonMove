The Innovation

Three proprietary technologies. One philosophy: your body deserves engineering, not marketing.

01

BodyMap™

Multi-Zone Adaptive Compression

Three to four strategically placed compression zones mapped to the body. Higher compression around the core and glutes for support and sculpting. Lighter compression at the calves and arms for freedom of movement. Breathable zones at high-heat areas. Designed with input from sports physiotherapists.

02

MineralSoft™

Recovery-Infused Fabric

Mineral-infused microcapsules release trace minerals through skin contact. Far-infrared yarn technology reflects body heat back as restorative energy. Anti-microbial silver-ion properties keep the garment fresh between washes. Wrapped in a matte luxury finish that looks as expensive as it performs.

03

ClimateKnit™

Temperature-Regulating Technology

Phase-change material microcapsules embedded in the fabric absorb body heat during intense exercise and release it when you cool down. This technology exists in outdoor and military gear — Maison Move brings it to premium women's activewear.

The Story

Maison Move is a house built for the body in motion. Founded by Sapph in Wales, it's a premium activewear and loungewear label that fuses fashion-house design with genuine fabric innovation.

The name: Maison because this is a house of design, not a warehouse of product. Move because that's what she does — constantly, confidently, beautifully.

Maison Move exists for the woman who doesn't separate her wardrobe into "gym" and "life." She moves through both with the same confidence, the same standards, and the same refusal to compromise.

The house was built for her.
